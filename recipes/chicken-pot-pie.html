Chicken Pot Pie

Description

A delicious chicken pie made from scratch with carrots, peas, and celery in a pre-made crust. Add thyme and poultry seasoning for more flavour.

Every home cook needs a good chicken pot pie recipe in their repertoire. On the hunt for a new family favourite? You're in luck! This homemade chicken pot pie definitely deserves a permanent spot in your recipe box.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts (cut into cubes) are ideal for making classic chicken pot pie.
  • Vegetables: This chicken pot pie recipe calls for the usual suspects: peas, carrots, onions, and celery. You could toss in some mushrooms or green peppers if you want to incorporate more veggies.
  • Butter and Flour: The chicken pot pie filling starts with a roux made with butter and all-purpose flour.
  • Seasoning: Achieve the perfect cosy flavour with salt, pepper, and celery seed. Of course, you can add more seasoning to suit your taste.
  • Broth and Milk: Chicken broth and milk add depth of flavour and help create a smooth and decadent chicken pot pie filling consistency.
